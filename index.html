07  —  Fireplace

Light
the fire.

The fireplace is one of the best features of the house. Follow these steps and you'll have it going in minutes.

  • 1

    Confirm the damper is fully open. Reach up and pull the handle toward you until it stops.

    You should feel air drafting upward when it's open.

  • 2

    Place two fire starters on the grate and stack three or four pieces of seasoned wood loosely on top.

  • 3

    Light the fire starters from below using the long lighter on the mantle.

  • 4

    Leave the glass doors open until the fire is well established — about 10 minutes — then close them.

    Closing the doors reduces smoke and increases efficiency.

  • 5

    When done, allow the fire to burn out completely. Never leave unattended. Close the damper once fully cool.

08  —  House Rules

How we
share this
space.

A few simple expectations that help us keep this house in great shape for every guest.

  • 01

    No smoking indoors. The deck is fine — please dispose of cigarettes in the sand bucket provided.

  • 02

    Pets are welcome with prior approval. Please keep them off the furniture and clean up after them outside.

  • 03

    Quiet hours after 10pm. The neighbors are close and the walls are thin.

  • 04

    Maximum occupancy is six guests. Please do not host additional visitors overnight.

  • 05

    Leave the kitchen as you found it. Run the dishwasher before checkout if you've used it.

  • 06

    Sand stays outside. Rinse off at the outdoor shower before coming in from the beach.
